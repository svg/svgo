Does not remove <script> elements even when script is declared with an explicit
namespace that isn't default SVG, or the explicit SVG or XHTML namespace.

Our custom namespace should be treated separately by clients and is not
executable.

===

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" xmlns:filmmaker="http://teaparty.diy" viewBox="0 0 100 100" version="1.1">
  <script>
    alert('uwu');
  </script>
  <filmmaker:script>
    This is not executable JavaScript!
  </filmmaker:script>
</svg>

@@@
