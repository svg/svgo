Combine own child transform and inherited

===

<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg">
    <g attr1="val1">
        <g transform="rotate(45)">
            <path transform="scale(2)" d="..."/>
        </g>
        <path d="..."/>
    </g>
</svg>
